How I work

Embodied presence, honest reflection, lived integration.

Every woman arrives with her own history, rhythm, and readiness — there is no script. My role is to offer attuned presence, clear reflection, and a grounded space where what is emerging can be met and integrated.

  • Embodied presence — body, breath, nervous system. Insight becomes real when it is felt.
  • Honest reflection — clear, respectful mirrors; patterns named, truth-telling without judgment.
  • Integration — what arises is brought into daily life through practical steps.
