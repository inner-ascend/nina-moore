What this is

An entrance, not a program.

She Who Becomes is the open room of my practice — a community for women standing at a threshold, wherever you are on the way across it. You don't apply. You don't pay. You come in, and you're met.

Writing, and the work of becoming — kept in the open, so no woman has to arrive alone.

Some women stay here for the letters and the gatherings. Some, when the time is right, walk through into deeper work. Either way, this is where it begins — free, and yours for as long as you want it.

Everything here is free.

Four things live inside the community, and none of them ask for a card. Take what you need, when you need it — the room stays open.
